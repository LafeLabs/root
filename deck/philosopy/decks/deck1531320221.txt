    <div class="content"><h1>Post Numeric Value</h1>

<p style="text-align:justify;">
    In a world of plenty, where the trash feed is the main source of materials, most "things" do not get their value from amounts of metal or oil but from how things are arranged.  Thus most value is in essense <em>data structures</em>.  This alters what it means for something to have value.  If value comes from metal, it can be added up with numbers. But if value comes from how things are arranged, geometry and topology offer much more useful tools for understanding how to exchange value.

</p>
